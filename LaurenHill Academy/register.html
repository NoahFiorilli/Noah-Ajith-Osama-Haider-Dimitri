How to Register

Register at LaurenHill Academy

All new registrations for students for the 2015-2016 school year will take start being accepted as of November 26, 2014. LaurenHill Academy also registers new students throughout the school year, however, acceptance depends on classroom availability.

Certificate of Eligibility

In order to qualify for English Instruction in Quebec Public Schools, students must meet certain eligibility criteria under the Charter of the French Language as set out by the Quebec government.

For more information on the Charter of the French Language, or to apply for a certificate of eligibility, please visit the registration information section of the EMSB Website at: www.emsb.qc.ca

All applications for the Certificate of Eligibility for Secondary students must be done at the English Montreal School Board Administration Building.

 

International Families

LaurenHill Academy welcomes students and their families to Canada from all over the world. Families living Quebec on a temporary basis (work or school visa) can be eligible to send their children to an English public school.
